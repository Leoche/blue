What is CLW?

Worship    Reflection    Small groups    Free Time    Electives    Games    Food

  • Christian camp for young people aged 13-17 years old. 

  • An opportunity to grow in faith and love for God. 
  • Electives designed to empower campers and provide an opportunity to praise God through the use of gifts and talents.
  • Inspiring worship - aimed at encouraging daily worship in young people.
  • Personal reflection time: An opportunity to become closer to God and learn more about Him through scripture. 
  • Insanely fun games and activities to form  new friends and have fun in a Christian community. 
  • Small group time to share ideas, discuss opinions in an age specific group.
