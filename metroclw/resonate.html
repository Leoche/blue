Resonate is an initiative that brings young Christians together from all around Adelaide.
We meet at different Churches and services throughout the year to worship God as a community. Resonate events provide a great opportunity to catch up with friends from camps, bible studies, small groups, school, youth, church, anywhere! Have an amazing time together and be encouraged, challenged and empoweredby your brothers and sisters in Christ.

 In an orchestra, when all the musicians play notes at the right frequency, they resonate together to create a beautiful symphony. We are so excited to see everyone meeting together in worship, praising our God as one, loving, supporting and spurring each other on (Hebrews 10:23-25), as God’s symphony. This vision is one reason for the name Resonate. Also, a resonating frequency often impacts other objects, causing them to resonate together. We believe that this initiative will bring churches together; forming a community that is able to impact other communities with God’s love.
