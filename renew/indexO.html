RENEW is a new state-wide young adults (over 18) camp created by Blueprint Ministries.

We want to be changed from the inside out - to be renewed! 

So here's what I want you to do, God helping you: Take your everyday, ordinary life—your sleeping, eating, going-to-work, and walking-around life—and place it before God as an offering. Embracing what God does for you is the best thing you can do for him. Don't become so well-adjusted to your culture that you fit into it without even thinking. Instead, fix your attention on God. You'll be changed from the inside out. Readily recognize what he wants from you, and quickly respond to it. Unlike the culture around you, always dragging you down to its level of immaturity, God brings the best out of you, develops well-formed maturity in you. Romans 12:1-2 (The Message).

The RENEW weekend looks a bit like a CLW squished into 2 days - it will be full on and it will be fun! Guest Speaker, Small Groups, Electives, Worship, Activities and and Op-Shop Formal Evening - what more could you want?

Once you register we will send you out a “Medical & Consent” form to complete and return.

Electives:
This year at RENEW we have 6 exciting electives, and you will be able to go to two of them. Three of the electives will help equip you to serve the church and other people, and three will help you grow in your relationships with God and other people. Please select your top 3 preferences indicating which electives you would like to go to, and we will do our best to give you two of your top 3 preferences. Please select your preferences during the online registration process.
