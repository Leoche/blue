About

 

Living by God’s design

Taking our name from a passage in Paul’s letter to the Ephesians, Blueprint Ministries is about encouraging generations of young believers to grow into, fulfil, and live out the design God has for us and our lives.

“It's in Christ that we find out who we are and what we are living for. Long before we first heard of Christ and got our hopes up, he had his eye on us, had designs on us for glorious living, part of the overall purpose he is working out in everything and everyone.” Ephesians 1:11-12 The Message

Through our mission and ministry programs, our aim is to:

  • Ignite a passion for Jesus in young people who know their identity in Christ
  • Build lifelong Christian disciples who value following Gods call to care for His people, planet, and kingdom
  • Grow volunteers who are well supported, empowered and encouraged
  • Inspire change and the future church through a common purpose, focus and communication

Operating Context

Operating from the LCA SA/NT District Office, Blueprint Ministries works alongside other departments (Mission, Tertiary and Administration) to provide ministry programs in accordance with the LCA’s vision for the church.

Under the guidance of District Church Council and the Council for Child, Youth and Family Ministry (CYFM), Blueprint Ministries is responsible for several key ministry programs catering specifically to children, families, youth and young adults across the LCA SA/NT.
